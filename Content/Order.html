*****
ORDER:
              The Vice-Chancellor is pleased to constitute a Fact Finding Committee to visit the following Self-Financing Institution for Grant of Temporary Affiliation for the Academic Year 2018-19.
 
##TABLE##
 
            The Fact Finding Committee is requested to inspect the Institutions, verify the faculty , other staff , labs, library, class rooms and other infrastructure for the courses cited as per norms and furnish the report as per Format A-418 (UG & PG) along with deficiencies and remarks, if any. The committee is requested to submit the reports separately for each institution to the University in the enclosed envelope addressed to DAAC immediately after the inspection.
 
While providing all the details in Format A-418 (UG & PG), the FFC Members are requested to focus specially on the following items:
 
1. Details of Principal/Director, Faculty, Technical staff & other staff members-Department wise (Names, Qualification etc) physically available during the inspection along with ratification/ SCM status and verify the Biometric attendance.
2. Laboratory equipment and facilities as per syllabus: (list of UG &PG labs as per JNTUH curriculum).
3. Infrastructure Facilities: Built up area, Number of class rooms, Drawing halls, Seminar halls, Museums (in case of Pharmacy), Standby Generator, etc.
4. Library facilities: No of seats, Titles and Number of Books & Journals - department wise.
5. Total number of computers available with P4 and above configuration and Internet bandwidth facilities.
6. Feedback from Faculty and Students (Interaction in the absence of Principal/ Management or his representative).
7. Submission of the videography of the inspection visit in the form of CD along with Reports.
 
              The Fact Finding Committee members are eligible for sitting charges of Rs.4000/- per College visted. The University will provide the vehicle for inspection through an authorized travel agency. Please verify the meter reading and time of arrival and release of vehicle before signing.
 
              As the University is considering the Affiliation process seriously, all the members are requested to adjust their programmes to adhere to the inspection date. If any of the member(s) of the FFC team are not in a position to participate in the inspection due to unavoidable circumstances, he/she shall report in writing their inability with reasons to the Registrar, JNTUH.
 
              In case, on the day of inspection, the University Examinations are in progress at the affiliated College, you are requested to monitor the process of examination at the Centre with minimum disturbance to the examination system and to the students. In such a situation the details/availability of staff performing examination work needs to be accounted for while inspecting the college instead of calling for a separate meeting.
 
              The FFC members are requested to fill the Format A-418 (UG & PG) in all respects in ink and submit on the same day of the visit.
 
Note : Fact Finding Committee should verify Faculty (with provided faculty lists of UG and for each PG course separately) Infrastructure, Labs, Library, and other facilities related to the UG & PG courses, along with the necessary documents and evidences. Please collect all the information as per check list.
